1. EDGE

Overview

4. COMMUTER

Overview

This is a MVP developed during android's bootcamp at eMobilis. It is android app whose purpose is to connect the public transport sector with the commuters so as to reduce time wastage especially during rush hours. By acting as an intermediary between the transport sector and the commuters, the platform helps the drivers and touts of the public transport vehicles to locate bus stops that require their urgent attention.
